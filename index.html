Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our special day

What's the dress code for the wedding?

expand_more

We're requesting formal attire for our special day. Think elegant dresses for the ladies and sharp suits or dress shirts with ties for the gentlemen. We'd love to see you dressed up to celebrate with us!

What's the parking situation at the venues?

expand_more

Both venues have ample parking available. At San Roque Parish Church, you can park in the subdivision area. The Coco Palace has a large parking lot right at the venue.

Can I bring a plus-one?

expand_more

Please check your invitation for plus-one details. If you're unsure, feel free to reach out to us directly. We're happy to accommodate where possible!

What should I do if I have dietary restrictions?

expand_more

Please indicate any dietary restrictions or allergies in your RSVP form. We'll make sure our caterer can accommodate your needs so everyone can enjoy the celebration safely.

Will there be transportation between venues?

expand_more

The ceremony and reception venues are approximately 15 minutes apart. Many guests choose to drive their own vehicles or carpool. Ride-sharing services like Grab are also available in San Pablo City.

What time should I arrive?

expand_more

Please arrive at the church by 2:00 PM to be seated before the 2:30 PM ceremony begins. The reception will start immediately following the ceremony around 2:30 PM.

Can I take photos during the church ceremony?

expand_more

To respect the solemnity of the ceremony, we kindly ask that guests refrain from taking photos during the ceremony itself. Our professional photographer will capture all the special moments, and we'll be happy to share the photos with you afterward!

What's the best way to get to San Pablo City?

expand_more

San Pablo City is easily accessible by car via the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX). It's about 1.5-2 hours from Manila depending on traffic. You can also take buses from various terminals in Metro Manila.

Will there be accommodations for out-of-town guests?

expand_more

While we don't have a formal hotel block, there are several excellent hotels in San Pablo City including Hotel Palawan and various resorts. We've included a list of recommended accommodations in the venues section.
